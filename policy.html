📋 Ye Policy Kya Hai?

SheherSaathi ek free platform hai jo students aur naye logon ko naye sheher mein settle hone mein help karta hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum tumhara data kaise use karte hain aur tumhare rights kya hain.

📥 Hum Kya Data Collect Karte Hain?

  • Naam aur email — sirf login ke liye
  • PG listing details — jo tum khud daalte ho
  • Reviews aur ratings — tumhare dwara likhe gaye
  • Dark mode preference — local device pe save hota hai

Hum koi bhi sensitive data jaise bank details, Aadhaar, ya passwords collect nahi karte.

🔒 Data Kaise Safe Hai?

Tumhara data tumhare device ke localStorage mein save hota hai — kisi server pe upload nahi hota. Hum kisi third party ke saath tumhara data share nahi karte.

🍪 Cookies

Hum sirf localStorage use karte hain — cookies nahi. Iska matlab hai tumhara data browser band karne ke baad bhi safe rehta hai aur hum track nahi karte.
