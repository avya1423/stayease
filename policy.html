Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority — read how we protect your data

📅 Last Updated: January 2026

📋 About This Policy

SheherSaathi is a free platform that helps students and newcomers settle comfortably in a new city. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and what rights you have as a user.

📥 What Data We Collect

  • Name and email — used only for login and profile
  • Phone number — optional, for PG listing contact
  • PG listing details — submitted voluntarily by you
  • Reviews and ratings — written by you
  • Saved PGs and preferences — stored locally on your device

🔒 We do NOT collect bank details, Aadhaar numbers, passwords in plain text, or any payment information.

🔐 How Your Data Is Stored

All your data is stored in your browser's localStorage — it stays on your device and is NOT uploaded to any server. Your password is encrypted before storage. We do not use any external analytics or tracking services.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We use only localStorage (not cookies). This means your data remains private, you are not tracked across websites, and your data stays after closing the browser. We have no advertising trackers or third-party scripts.

🏠 PG Listing Policy

  • Listing your PG is completely free
  • Providing false contact information is not allowed
  • Misleading or fake listings will be removed upon report
  • We are not directly responsible for transactions between PG owners and tenants
  • Always visit and verify a PG before paying any advance

⭐ Reviews Policy

  • Reviews must be genuine and based on real experience
  • Abusive, hateful, or spam content is not allowed
  • Fake reviews can be reported — we will remove them
  • We reserve the right to moderate any content

👤 Your Rights

  • Delete your data anytime — clear your browser's localStorage
  • Remove your PG listing — contact us directly
  • Request data correction — email us anytime
  • Opt out — simply stop using the platform
